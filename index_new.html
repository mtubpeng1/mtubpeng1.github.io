📖 Biography

Bo Peng received his B.E. degree in Computer Science from Southwest Petroleum University (SWPU), Chengdu, China, in 2003, followed by an M.S. in 2007 and a Ph.D. in 2014 from Sichuan University, Chengdu, China. He joined SWPU as a Lecturer in 2007 and currently serves as a Professor of Computer Science.


Between 2014 and 2015, he pursued post-doctoral research in the Department of Biomedical Engineering at Michigan Technological University, Houghton, USA, under the supervision of Dr. Jingfeng Jiang. From 2020 to 2021, he was a Visiting Scholar at the Medical Ultrasound Engineering (MUSE) Lab in the Department of Biomedical Engineering, Tsinghua University School of Medicine, Beijing, China, working with Dr. Jianwen Luo (Chang Jiang Scholar Chair Professor).


Research Focus: Medical imaging technologies, with expertise in ultrasound elastography, ultrasound localization microscopy, GPU-accelerated computing, and medical image analysis. Recently expanded into machine learning, computer vision (petroleum industry and aerodynamics applications), and LLM quantification.
